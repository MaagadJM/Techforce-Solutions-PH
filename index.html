About Us

Techforce Solutions Inc. is a leading IT holding company, driving innovation and growth across the technology landscape. We specialize in identifying and acquiring high-potential companies in key IT sectors, providing them with the resources, expertise, and guidance to achieve their full potential. To reduce risk, pay as little tax as possible, increase returns, and protect privacy.

Our Investment Focus

Emerging Technologies:
We actively invest in companies developing and applying cutting-edge technologies like AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, blockchain, and the Internet of Things (IoT).

Software & Services:
We back businesses delivering innovative software solutions and professional services across various industries.

High-Growth Markets:
We target geographically diverse markets with significant potential for technological advancement and economic expansion.

What We Do

Strategic Acquisitions:
We leverage our expertise to identify and acquire promising IT companies with strong leadership teams and disruptive solutions.

Operational Excellence:
We provide our portfolio companies with access to capital, operational guidance, and industry expertise to accelerate their growth.

Value Creation:
We actively engage with our investee companies to unlock their full potential, maximizing value for our investors and stakeholders.

Our Impact

Driving Innovation:

Creating Jobs:

Delivering Value:
